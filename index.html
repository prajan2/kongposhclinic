Work Experience

Anusuya Datta is a Clinical Psychologist and Psychotherapist working in the field of mental health for the last 8 years. She has completed her M.Phil. from the Institute of Human Behaviour and Allied Sciences (IHBAS), Delhi. She is registered with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI) as a Clinical Psychologist. She has done her Graduation in Psychology, Sociology and Economics from Christ College bangalore and her Masters in Psychology from Montfort College bangalore. After her Masters she has worked in the Cognitive Neurology Unit of Apollo Hospital kolkata conducting neuropsychological assessments for geriatric and child clients. Anusuya primarily follows Cognitive Behaviour Therapy and is influenced by Narrative and Family Therapy in her approach. She believes that the client-therapist relationship is what drives change. She works with individuals above 15 years of age. Whenever she finds free time Anusuya enjoys catching up on shows. Anusuya fulfills her creative side by dabbling in cooking and baking.
